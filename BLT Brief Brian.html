Lemonade Rooftop Party
⏰ 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM
📍 Platform Sports, Charlotte NC
About This Event

When life gives you a rooftop, you throw Lemonade. Lemonade is an elevated rooftop experience where the fashion is as high as the view. This edition features Beyoncé's greatest hits woven through Hip Hop and R&B timeless classics. Yellow threads. Curated cocktails. Lemonade is THE rooftop moment of the summer — a golden-hour glow up where the drinks hit and the songs don't miss.

Theme

Beyoncé & Friends — Yellow Everything. City views, golden hour, and nothing but good energy. Guests arrive in yellow attire. Handcrafted cocktails. Photo-ready moments under the skyline.

Vision & Goal for Success

Year 2 of Lemonade builds on a sold-out first edition. The goal is not just to sell out again — it's to make guests feel the difference. This year we're elevating the full experience: a more premium VIP offering, intentional activations and photo moments, and dress code enforcement that makes the yellow theme real in the room. Hennessy returns as an activation partner. A successful Lemonade 2 proves the concept can travel.

Thursday, June 19, 2026 — Juneteenth
One Black A** Day Party (OBADP)
⏰ 3:00 PM – 9:00 PM
📍 Victoria Yards, Charlotte NC
About This Event

The BLT family reunion. A celebration of Black culture, creativity, and community. BLT's Juneteenth signature event — a high-energy day party built around live DJ sets, curated programming, local vendors, small businesses, and creatives. Rooted in honoring the full Black experience — the music, art, food, fashion, and more. Every element is intentionally curated to reflect and celebrate Black excellence.

Theme

Juneteenth — Black culture, creativity, and community. A full-day block party experience built for 2,000 people at Victoria Yards.

Vision & Goal for Success

Year 3 of OBADP makes its biggest leap yet — first block party format at Victoria Yards, targeting 2,000 attendees. A sold-out, well-executed OBADP 2026 plants the flag for Charlotte's definitive Juneteenth event and serves as proof of concept for taking OBADP on the road as part of BLT's summer tour.

About This Event

Legends Never Die is the only true R&B party in Charlotte. We're paying homage to the R&B and Neo-soul legends of all eras — giving them their flowers for their contributions. Let's reminisce a little. They say they don't make 'em like they used to. Nothing but hot, soulful, heart-throbbing R&B and Neo-soul. Deep in the cut. No Rap. No Trap. It's really R&B only.

Theme

Grown and Sexy. 30+ only. Some of the best R&B DJs specifically selected to set the mood just right. Indoor + outdoor experience at a new venue for BLT.

Vision & Goal for Success

LND 30+ is BLT's signature grown and sexy experience — an intimate, curated night for a more mature crowd. At 450 attendees, the goal is a sold-out, high-energy room where every song hits and the vibe never breaks. Success here proves LND can become a recurring concept across multiple markets as part of the summer tour.
