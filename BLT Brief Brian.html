Lemonade Rooftop Party
About This Event

When life gives you a rooftop, you throw Lemonade. Lemonade is an elevated rooftop experience where the fashion is as high as the view. This edition features Beyoncé's greatest hits woven through Hip Hop and R&B timeless classics. Yellow threads. Curated cocktails. Lemonade is THE rooftop moment of the summer — a golden-hour glow up where the drinks hit and the songs don't miss.

Theme

Beyoncé & Friends — Yellow Everything. City views, golden hour, and nothing but good energy. Guests arrive in yellow attire. Handcrafted cocktails. Photo-ready moments under the skyline.

Vision & Goal for Success

Year 2 of Lemonade builds on a sold-out first edition. The goal is not just to sell out again — it's to make guests feel the difference. This year we're elevating the full experience: a more premium VIP offering, intentional activations and photo moments, and dress code enforcement that makes the yellow theme real in the room. Hennessy returns as an activation partner. A successful Lemonade 2 proves the concept can travel and sets the foundation for Lemonade as BLT's signature annual rooftop property.

Thursday, June 19, 2026 — Juneteenth • Victoria Yards, Charlotte NC
One Black A** Day Party (OBADP)
About This Event

The BLT family reunion. A celebration of Black culture, creativity, and community. BLT's Juneteenth signature event — a high-energy day party built around live DJ sets, curated programming, local vendors, small businesses, and creatives. Rooted in honoring the full Black experience — the music, art, food, fashion, and more. Every element is intentionally curated to reflect and celebrate Black excellence. Designed to be both a cultural experience and a good time, the event creates intentional space for connection to community.

Theme

Juneteenth — Black culture, creativity, and community. A full-day block party experience built for 2,000 people at Victoria Yards.

Vision & Goal for Success

Year 3 of OBADP makes its biggest leap yet — its first block party format at Victoria Yards, targeting 2,000 attendees. A sold-out, well-executed OBADP 2026 plants the flag for Charlotte's definitive Juneteenth event and serves as proof of concept for taking OBADP on the road as part of BLT's summer tour.
